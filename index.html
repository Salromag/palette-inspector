Privacy Policy

Palette Inspector – Color Palette Extractor does not collect, store, or share any personal information or browsing data.

All processing is done locally in your browser when you click the analyze button. No information is sent to any server or third party.

This extension only accesses the content of the current page temporarily to extract color data, and only when the user initiates the analysis.
